About Us

Al-Tibyan Quran Academy

We are dedicated to spreading authentic Islamic knowledge through articles, lectures, and Quranic education.

Our mission is to provide easy access to Islamic learning for Muslims around the world. Founded by Hafiz Faheem Firdosi, our academy focuses on teaching the Quran with proper Tajweed, explaining Hadith, and clarifying Islamic rulings according to the Quran and Sunnah.

With over 100 articles on various Islamic topics, we aim to be a comprehensive resource for Muslims seeking knowledge.
